# Contributor License Agreement (CLA) Policy

This repository follows a lightweight CLA policy for all contributions.

## Policy

By contributing to this repository (code, docs, tests, workflows, or any
other material), you agree that:

1. You have the legal right to submit the contribution.
2. The contribution is your original work, or you have proper rights to
   submit it.
3. You grant the repository maintainers and copyright holder a perpetual,
   worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use, modify, distribute,
   sublicense, and relicense your contribution as part of this project.

## How To Accept

Submitting a Pull Request is considered acceptance of this CLA policy.

For external contributors, maintainers may request an explicit confirmation in
the PR description:

- `I have read and agree to CLA.md`

## Corporate Contributions

If you contribute on behalf of an organization, ensure your organization
authorizes this contribution under the same terms.

## Notes

- This CLA policy works together with `LICENSE`, `CONTRIBUTING.md`, and
  `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`.
- Security reports should follow `SECURITY.md`.
